New Age Banking

Ekagrata Finance Pvt. Ltd. is the NBFC entity registered with the Reserve Bank of India. Ekagrata Finance is a 100% subsidiary of the Bengaluru-based FinTech company Datasigns Technologies which owns the brand name Shubh Loans, a key online lending platform in India.

Shubh Loans is a value-added, lending solutions platform that provides credit in simple steps to the unserved and underserved segments in India. The platform leverages data sciences to mitigate risks and improve customer experiences during loan application. Applicants will also avail the proprietorial credit score that is built in-house for Shubh Loans for quicker access to credit. With easy EMIs and longer tenures, the platform is a forerunner of financial inclusiveness in India.

As Shubh Loans widens its presence in more Indian cities, Ekagrata Finance plays a pivotal role as its NBFC arm in co-lending and providing value added products to its offering.

Redefining Credit

Ekagrata Finance is redefining lending by using proprietary credit scoring algorithms to not only assess a person's ability but also intent to repay.

Flexible Products

Ekagrata Finance is enabling Shubh Loans to test new segments/markets and also offer risk based flexible pricing products.

Customer Focused

We want our customers to get quick and hassle free loans when the need arises, whether it is for medical use, paying education bills or during any other exigencies.
